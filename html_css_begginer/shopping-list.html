Steps to Brew a Cup of Coffee:

  1. Boil water.
  2. Grind the coffee beans.
  3. Place the coffee filter in the dripper.
  4. Add the ground coffee.
  5. Pour hot water slowly in circles.
  6. Let it drip completely.
